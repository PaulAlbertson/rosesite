Text: Publicis Groupe (link: / text: customer engagement agency) Rosetta today announced it has been named Minds Matter Cleveland’s 2014 Partner of the Year. The agency will be recognized at the charity’s annual fundraising gala scheduled to take place October 11 at the Cleveland Botanical Gardens. 

“Rosetta is a long-standing supporter of our organization and we’re thrilled to recognize their continued impact at this year’s event,” said President of Minds Matter Cleveland Chad Hartup. “They’ve played an instrumental role in the growth of Minds Matter Cleveland, allowing us to make a substantial impact on local teens pursuing a higher education.” 

Minds Matter of Cleveland is an official chapter of the national Minds Matter organization. Founded in 2008, the Cleveland chapter supports more than 60 students locally and has 175 volunteers in Northeast Ohio. With chapters across the country, the organization strives to transform the lives of high-performing high school students from low-income families by helping them achieve academic excellence and gain admission to leading colleges and universities.

“We are honored to be named the 2014 Partner of the Year,” said Rosetta’s Chief Talent Officer Nigel Adams. “Rosetta Cleveland is passionate about making an impact in the community, and we’re proud to partner with a group dedicated to helping Cleveland’s youth prosper.” 

The organization’s second annual charity dinner, Discovery Night, will be held at the Cleveland Botanical Gardens. Cocktails, hors d’oeuvres and discover the gardens will start at 6:30 p.m., and dinner will immediately follow at 7:30 p.m. A silent auction will be held to raise funding for future student programs.
