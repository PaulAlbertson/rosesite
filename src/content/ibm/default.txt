<h2 class="hide-br">A Customer Engagement Ecosystem</h2>

<p>More than five years ago, Rosetta introduced its Engagement Engine, which integrates customer information and interactions across all major touch points and channels, including: commerce, direct, media, in-store, online, customer relationship management (CRM) programs and call centers. Now Rosetta is building on its strong partnership with IBM to add a comprehensive suite of IBM Commerce solutions to the Engagement Engine, including: WebSphere Commerce, Sterling Order Management, Experience One, as well as IBM’s Big Data and Analytics solutions like Predictive Customer Intelligence, MDM, Big Insights, SPSS and more.</p>

<p>Brands can now deliver a new level of relevant, targeted and personalized experiences to their customers to drive deeper engagement.</p>
